This is an advanced undergraduate course aimed at giving a practical understanding of general relativity. The pedagogical style is to quickly arrive at calculating orbits and trajectories in curved spacetime. The derivation of the non-linear partial differential equations which specify how such a spacetime is sourced by matter and energy is left until the end, after the student has intuition about their solutions in the test-particle regime. See the syllabus for details regarding grading and supplementary texts.
